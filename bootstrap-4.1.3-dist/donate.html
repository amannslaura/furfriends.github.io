Donation Options

Fur Friends appreciates everyone's extreme generousity over the years in keeping the shelter up and running. Without our wonderful shared community of caring for animals, many pets might never have known the love and care that a forever home can provide. That would have meant a lot less tail wagging and purring in the world! Listed below is a list of donations that Fur Friends are always happy to accept. We cannot do this alone and appreciate your generosity. Thank you in advance for all your support!


List of items Fur Friends is always in need of:

  • Paper Products
  • Cleaning supplies
  • Leashes
  • Pet beds
  • Towels
  • Blankets
  • Kennels
  • Pet toys
  • Food

Please help support Fur Friends and their goal of finding every animal their forever home.
