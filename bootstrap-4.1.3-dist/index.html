Welcome to Forever Friends


We are passionate about helping animals in finding a home to receive the love and care they all deserve. We strive to find every animal we come in contact with in finding their forever home. When you take an animal in to your heart and home to be a part of your family, you have made a friend for life!


Featured Dog of the Month
-Tristan


Tristan was found wandering outside of a restaurant in the bitter days of winter looking for scraps. He was underweight and malnourished. Those days are a distant memory. He loves to play and will walk with anyone who is holding a leash. He is now searching for a walking companion for life.



Featured Cat of the Month
–Bunny


We know it's funny to name a cat Bunny, but as soon as you see her hop (or spring) you will be convinced too, that she has the right name. She came to Forever Friends two months ago with a severe eye infection. She is completely healed and ready to find a home she can claim for a lifetime.




If interested in adoptng either of our featured pets, please download an application form found on our adoption policy page.
