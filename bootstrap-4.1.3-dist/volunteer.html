Volunteering Possibilites

At Forever Friends there are multiple ways of giving a helping hand. Cleaning the pens, giving baths, walking the dogs, and helping to transport animals are just a few ways of assisting in the abundance of care these animals require until they find their forever home.

We are always on the search for volunteers that love animals and aren't afraid to get their hands dirty. Why not consider volunteering today! Please contact Heather our shelter manager at: 802-878-1758 with avilable times you have. We appreciate the time and devotion all our volunteers put forth in making many animals' lives better. We would love to have you join our team. We assure you, that you will make new friends—including furry ones!

Aaron

-Lisa

Featured Volunteer of the Month

From time to time we also get pets that have lost their older caregivers and are not used to being on their own. If interested please reach out to Heather at: 802-878-1758.
