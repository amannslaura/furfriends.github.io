Cats Up For Adoption

If interested in adopting any of the cats or kittens you see today please view our pet adoption policy.

All cats are spayed and neutered before being available for adoption.


Oreo

If you're looking for a cat with spunk, look no further! Oreo is dog friendly, in fact he may think he is one. Playing hide and seek is his favorite sport. He loves to sneak up on people for pets!

Sex: Male

Age: Two years

Gilbert

Gilbert is a playful four year old cat. He will do anything for his favorite treats. Even fetch a ball! This handsome guy will entertain you for hours! He loves people and other cats.

Sex: Male

Age: Four years

Rocky

Rocky came to us after losing his caregiver to an illness. He's slowly warming to others who want to provide a new home for him. A little shy at first, but very affectionate.

Sex: Male

Age: Three years

Iris and Tulip

Iris and Tulip came to us about a month ago. They are inseperable and want to find a forever home that will take them both. They are snuggly and love to play with toys.

Sex: Female

Age: One year

Rory

Rory was found outside an apartment complex thought to have been left behind when someone moved. She is definitely a lap cat that wants to be petted and shown attention.

Sex: Female

Age: Two years

Willow

Willow is a timid cat at first, until she gets to know you. Then she's your best friend. She loves curling up on the couch for a nap. Her blue eyes are stunning, she will find a forever home fast.

Sex: Female

Age: One year

Phoebe

Phoebe is a kitten who loves to make people laugh. She is always popping up in strange places. Her energy is never ending and loves to play with other cats. She's six months old and very energetic.

Sex: Female

Age: Six months

Midnight

Midnight was found in a storm drain when his eyes were barely open. He was bottle fed by his foster family and is ready to find his forever home. He enjoys getting into mischief!

Sex: Male

Age: Three months

Tofu

What can you say about Tofu, he's a ball of pure fluff! He is a cuddler and wants to play constantly, that is until it is time for a nap. Ask about adotion today for this fluffball!

Sex: Male

Age: Four months
