Fur Friends Dog Walk Fundraiser

Saturday April 29, 2023
Saxonhollow Park


With the coming of spring Fur Friends looks forward to the fresh spring air. What better way to enjoy the beautiful blooming spring flowers, than to walk with our four legged companions. Whether you want to run, walk, or crawl, you will be able to help benefit those in our community that need our help the most—the ones that don't have a voice and that depend on us to help provide them with care and help.There will be light refreshments afterwards with music by the Playful Pups band. Come for the walk—stay for the fun. Fur Friends will be bringing some of their furry friends that you can meet and greet with that will be up for adoption.



Link to Runbrite event page for Fur Friends Dog Walk Fundraiser signup form will be posted March 1, 2023.

Please check back! We're looking forward to seeing you there!
