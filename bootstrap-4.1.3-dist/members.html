At Forever Friends we work very hard in trying to provide a forever home for every animal that is in need of one. We carefully review each application to find the right fit for each family/individual and potential pet adoptee. Fur Friends appreciates the hard work and devotion our staff, community members, and many volunteers put forth in providing these opportunities for these animals that may have faced a different path in life. If interested in volunteering please reach out to Maddox Blundell at: 802-878-1758 to discuss availibility and current shelter needs.
